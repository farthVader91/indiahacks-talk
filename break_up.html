Scheduling Primer:
==================

  • Schedule computational elements(such as threads, processes, etc) onto hardware resources(such as processors, network links, etc).
    (explain briefly about ready queue)

  • Long term scheduler:
    aka Job Scheduler. Determines which jobs are to be pushed onto Ready Queue(in main memory) and loads them.

  • Medium term scheduler:
    Responsible for swapping processes from main memory and placing them in secondary storage.

  • Short term scheduler:
    aka CPU scheduler. Allocates CPU to processes from Ready Queue. Happens during interrupts(clock, i/o), system calls, signals. Hence more frequent.
